About SPINNO

In March 2021, when our Founder and CTO was a volunteering instructor at a middle school in East Africa, he realized the need for instructors' academic performance analysis work to be simpler. Thus, in a bid to help them free up their time and dedicate it to creating strategies for academic success, for their students, he discovered SPIN-ED, an academic performance management system.


The idea behind SPIN-ED was "one entry, multiple information," in other words, the tool was meant to be used by even the least tech-savvy instructor, by allowing them to enter one piece of data in the form of academic scores, which would automatically yield grade analysis aspects such as: the average score for each student and the entire class, weighted averages for each student, the position of each student in a particular subject and the letter grades. Additionally, the "one entry" that an instructor enters would auto-generate a report card, for every student, with automated comments, that can be adjusted by the instructor. Moreover, the program would automatically graph the averages of each student to indicate a performance trend.


So, why use SPIN-ED?

It is user-friendly and can be used to:

  • Simplify student performance evaluation tasks
  • Increase accuracy, precision, objectivity and fairness in student performance evaluation
  • Reduce time spent in student performance evaluation tasks
  • Facilitate ongoing research in education
  • Serve as an academic performance management system
